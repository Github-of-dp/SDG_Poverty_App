SDG-1: No Poverty

AI-Based Poverty Risk Estimation

⚠️ Educational Use Only
This tool provides an estimate based on simplified economic indicators. Income is adjusted using country-specific poverty thresholds to reduce regional bias.

How this works:
• Income is normalized using official poverty thresholds
• A logistic regression model estimates poverty risk probability
• Results vary by country due to cost-of-living differences

This estimate does not determine eligibility for benefits and should not be used for policy decisions.
