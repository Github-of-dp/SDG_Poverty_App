What is SDG-1?

Sustainable Development Goal 1 aims to end poverty in all its forms everywhere. Poverty is not just a lack of income; it includes a lack of basic services, such as education, health, and digital access. This AI model simulates how these "Multidimensional" factors combine to determine a household's vulnerability.

How the Model Works

We use a Logistic Regression algorithm. It assigns a "weight" to each life factor. Income and Education act as "Protective Factors" (negative weights), while large Family Sizes in resource-scarce environments act as "Risk Factors" (positive weights).
